Affiliate Manager
Affiliate Manager Specialist
Strategic Focus
  • Execute + Optimize
Main Function
  • Manage affiliate programs and drive revenue partnerships
Core Responsibilities
  • Help clients set-up new affiliate programs, or audit clients' existing affiliate programs, and advise on and execute best practices
  • Manage program approvals / rejections for multiple clients
  • Pitch publication affiliate managers new products, sales, best-sellers, et al
  • Negotiate with publication affiliate managers on commission rates, list rankings, and adding clients to existing articles
  • Provide clients with detailed yet easy-to-understand weekly reporting that includes revenue generated, commissions paid, click activity, and top partners

Success Metrics
  • A measurable increase in monthly PR placements for our clients in tier 1 publications
  • A measurable increase in direct affiliate partnerships established with tier 1 publications
  • A measurable increase in the percentage of revenue driven by PR placements for our clients
  • Effectively and expeditiously communicating with clients and team members
